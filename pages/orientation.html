My Orientation Week At Refactory

Orientation is important because it lays a foundation for the new student's entire career with the school. First impressions are important since they establish the basis for everything that follows. Without orientation, a new student sometimes feels uncomfortable in his/her new environment and takes longer to reach his/her full potential.

My orientation week at Refactory was awesome. It was held via Zoom. I had the opportunity to meet with different facilitators, alumnis and friends. My expectations were made clear and objectives of the program were all clarified.

I was also introduced to the tools that we were going to using during the program. Tools covered include Visual Studio Code ( a text editor), Git (A version control tool).
